Education

Here at RE-invent student’s platform we focus on the youth and undergraduate’s development. We seek to provide students with skills and knowledge they can gain from the multiple platforms, projects, and interactions with others. So, to enable their innovation, creativity and get the exposure to the corporate world for work and collaborative opportunities.

Evaluation

At RE-invent would like to empower and up-skills the youth, but fist we need to do evaluations on their compatibility in every field that they might be interested in signing up or applying for. As this will increase the rate of people following their passion and low the rate of dropouts in our institutions. Therefore this is a platform whereby we do all the assessments we will look at things like evaluation tests, psychological and education tests. These tests are being done so that we can have level of understanding about students.
